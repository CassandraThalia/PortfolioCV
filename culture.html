 VISITOR RESEARCH REPORT 

GARDINER MUSEUM OF CERAMICS, 2018

As part of a team of 6 people, I was lucky enough to work with Toronto's Gardiner Museum of Ceramics to perform a summative visitor research report of their new Steven Heinemann exhibition.
We collected the data by observing & interviewing museum visitors and presented this report to the museum board. It was a big effort and our team was extremely proud of our results. To date it is one of my favourite projects I have ever worked on.
